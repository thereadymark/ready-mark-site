Privacy Policy

The Ready Mark is committed to protecting the privacy of guests, clients, and users of our verification and reporting platform.

Effective Date: March 30, 2026
Last Updated:March 30, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how The Ready Mark collects, uses, stores, and protects information submitted through our website, verification portal, guest reporting system, and related services.

1. Information We Collect

We may collect the following information when you use our services:

  • Name
  • Email address
  • Verification and login information
  • Property and room-related reporting details
  • Photos or supporting documents voluntarily submitted by a guest
  • Technical usage information such as browser, device, pages viewed, and timestamps

2. How We Use Information

We use collected information only for legitimate operational purposes, including:

  • Creating and managing guest accounts
  • Sending verification codes and account-related emails
  • Receiving, logging, and managing cleanliness or service-related reports
  • Communicating submitted issues to the relevant property or hotel team
  • Improving system functionality, service quality, and user experience
  • Maintaining audit records, verification records, and platform security

3. Our Privacy Commitment

The Ready Mark does not sell guest information.

We do not rent guest information.

We do not share personal information with third parties for marketing purposes.

Information submitted through our platform is used only as necessary to operate the verification and reporting system, support issue resolution, and communicate with the relevant property when a guest chooses to submit a report.

4. Limited Sharing of Information

When a guest submits a report through The Ready Mark, certain information may be shared with the relevant property, hotel, or client for the purpose of reviewing and responding to the reported issue. This may include:

  • The guest’s name
  • The guest’s email address
  • Reported issue details
  • Submitted photos or supporting materials
  • Timestamps, room information, and reference numbers

This information is shared only as needed for issue review, identity confirmation, response, remediation, quality assurance, and dispute documentation.

5. Data Protection and Storage

We take reasonable administrative, technical, and operational measures to protect information submitted to our platform. While no digital platform can guarantee absolute security, we work to store information using secure systems and restricted access practices appropriate to the nature of the service.

6. Verification Emails and Communications

By creating an account or using our guest reporting system, you agree to receive necessary operational emails from The Ready Mark, including:

  • Email verification codes
  • Confirmation messages
  • Issue-related follow-up communications
  • Important system or service notices

We do not use these communications to send unrelated promotional marketing unless separately authorized.

7. Data Retention

We retain submitted information only for as long as reasonably necessary to operate the service, maintain records, support investigations, document issue history, comply with legal obligations, and protect the integrity of the platform.

8. User Responsibility

Guests should only submit truthful, accurate, and relevant information. By using the platform, users acknowledge that submitted reports may be reviewed as part of property response, service resolution, and internal documentation processes.

9. Children’s Privacy

The Ready Mark platform is not intended for use by children under the age of 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

10. Updates to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes to our services, systems, or legal obligations. Updated versions will be posted on this page with a revised effective date.
